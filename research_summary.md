# Research Summary

## Research Question
How does predictive model performance vary across different market uncertainty regimes?

## Motivation
Financial markets are non-stationary, and model accuracy may not be stable across different volatility environments.

## Methodology
This project compares machine learning models under different regimes using benchmark comparisons, overfitting checks, and rolling-window validation.

## Main Findings
Models may perform differently across low-, medium-, and high-volatility regimes. Aggregate accuracy alone is insufficient for evaluating model reliability.

## Relevance to Operations Management
The same framework can be applied to demand forecasting, staffing prediction, service operations, and capacity planning under uncertainty.
