# Methodology

## Data
Describe the market data frequency and variables.

## Feature Engineering
Lagged returns, rolling volatility, momentum, volume features, and volatility proxy.

## Regime Definition
Low-, medium-, and high-volatility regimes are constructed using volatility quantiles.

## Models
Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, benchmarks.

## Evaluation
Train-test split, overfitting gap, regime-specific accuracy, rolling-window validation.

## Robustness Checks
Random-walk benchmark and rolling-window stability analysis.
